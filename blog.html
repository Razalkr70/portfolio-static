AI Transforming the Future of Healthcare

May 10, 2025 • AI & Healthcare

Artificial Intelligence is reshaping the healthcare industry by introducing data-driven approaches to diagnosis, drug discovery, and personalized treatment. With machine learning models analyzing vast datasets, AI aids in early detection of diseases like cancer and Alzheimer’s, potentially saving millions of lives.

In the near future, AI-powered wearable devices and virtual assistants could also revolutionize how patients manage chronic conditions and medication schedules.

Creating a Dynamic Sales Dashboard with Power BI

Mar 30, 2025 • Data Visualization

This blog explores a practical data visualization project where we built a dynamic and interactive Sales Dashboard using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard effectively displays key performance indicators (KPIs), sales trends, regional performance, and customer insights, providing business leaders with actionable intelligence.

We covered data connection from multiple sources (Excel, SQL), Power Query transformations, DAX calculations for metrics, and the design of a user-friendly, visually compelling report. Finally, the dashboard was shared via the Power BI Service for real-time updates and collaboration.
